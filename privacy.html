AFOM Solutions Pvt Ltd, operating under the brand name Unstuck ("we," "us," or "our"), operates the Unstuck mobile application (the "App") through its website unstuckforlife.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use our App. AFOM Solutions Pvt Ltd is the data controller for your personal information. Unstuck is a mental health support application that provides guided audio sessions, daily journaling tools, and exposure therapy features to help users manage anxiety, panic attacks, and intrusive thoughts.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when you create an account and use the App:

  • Account Information: Email address (required for authentication), display name, gender, and profile photo.
  • Journal Entries: Mood ratings, anxiety levels, sleep quality, energy levels, and any free-text reflections you record in your daily journal.
  • Exposure Therapy Logs: Records of your exposure therapy sessions, including anxiety ratings (SUDS scores), session notes, hierarchy items, and progress data.
  • Medications: Medication name, dosage, schedule, and optional notes recorded in the medication-tracker feature.
  • Habits: Habit name, emoji, schedule, and log entries recorded in the habit-tracker feature.
  • Playlists and Saved Tracks: Audio content you save, favorite, or organize into playlists within the App.
  • Profile Photo: An optional avatar image you upload to personalize your account.
  • Microphone Audio (Transient): If you use voice-to-text input for journal or exposure logs, audio is processed entirely on-device by your device's built-in speech recognizer (Apple's Speech framework on iOS, Android's on-device SpeechRecognizer on Android). The app forces on-device recognition and will not send audio to Apple or Google's cloud services. On devices that don't support on-device speech recognition, voice input is unavailable and you'll see an in-app message asking you to type instead. We do not store or transmit raw audio.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we may automatically collect certain technical information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers necessary for push notification delivery.
  • Push Notification Tokens: FCM token (Android), APNs token (iOS), and OneSignal player ID — used to deliver notifications such as reminders and re-engagement messages. Not shared with any party other than OneSignal.
  • Usage Data: App feature usage patterns, session duration, and interaction data to improve our services.
  • Log Data: Error logs, crash reports, stack traces, and breadcrumbs (no PII) to diagnose and fix technical issues. Reported via Firebase Crashlytics.

We do not currently use any third-party analytics services beyond crash and exception reporting. If we add analytics in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly.

1.3 Information from Third-Party Authentication

If you choose to sign in using a third-party service, we receive limited information from that provider:

  • Google OAuth: Your name, email address, and profile picture associated with your Google account.
  • Facebook/Meta OAuth: Your name, email address, and profile picture associated with your Facebook account.
  • Apple Sign-In: Your name and email address (or a private relay email address if you choose to hide your email) associated with your Apple ID.

We do not receive or store your passwords from any third-party authentication provider.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide and Maintain the App: To create and manage your account, deliver the core features of the App (guided audio, journaling, exposure therapy), and personalize your experience.
  • Authentication: To verify your identity when you sign in via email, Google, Facebook, or Apple.
  • Improve Our Services: To understand how users interact with the App and identify areas for improvement, bug fixes, and new feature development.
  • Communications: To send you important service-related notifications, such as security alerts, account verification messages, and updates to our terms or policies.
  • Safety and Security: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, and abuse.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

We do not use your journal entries, exposure therapy logs, or other mental health data for advertising, marketing, or any purpose other than providing and improving the App's core functionality for you.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

We use trusted third-party service providers to operate and support the App. These providers have access to your information only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are contractually obligated to protect your data:

Your account data, journal entries, exposure therapy logs, and uploaded files (audio, profile photos) are stored on Supabase servers located in the US East region. Supabase processes data in accordance with their privacy policy and data processing agreements.

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to:

  • Comply with a legal obligation, court order, or legal process.
  • Protect and defend our rights or property.

  • Protect the personal safety of users of the App or the public.
  • Protect against legal liability.

If Unstuck is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of its assets, your personal information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email or prominent notice within the App before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure located in the US East region. We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS/SSL protocols.
  • Encryption of data at rest on our database servers.
  • Row-level security policies on our database to ensure users can only access their own data.
  • Secure authentication via one-time passcodes (OTP), OAuth tokens, and JSON Web Tokens (JWT).
  • Regular security reviews of our infrastructure and access controls.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. Specifically:

  • Account Data: Retained for the duration of your active account.
  • Journal Entries and Exposure Logs: Retained for the duration of your active account so you can access your historical data and track your progress over time.
  • Uploaded Files: Profile photos and other uploaded content are retained for the duration of your active account.
  • Technical Logs: Crash reports and error logs are retained for up to 90 days for debugging purposes.

When you delete your account, all of your personal data, including journal entries, exposure therapy logs, playlists, saved tracks, profile photos, and account information, is permanently deleted from our systems. This deletion is irreversible.

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can access your personal data at any time through the App's profile and settings screens.
  • Correction: You can update or correct your account information (name, email, gender, profile photo) through the App's settings.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the App's account settings. Upon deletion, all your data is permanently removed from our servers.
  • Data Export: You can request a copy of your personal data by contacting us at hello@unstuckforlife.com.
  • Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time by contacting us or adjusting your settings within the App.
  • Opt Out of Communications: You can manage your notification preferences within the App's settings or by contacting us.
