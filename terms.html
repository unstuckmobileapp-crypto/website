2. Description of the Service

Unstuck is an anxiety and panic support application designed to provide self-help tools and educational resources. The Service includes:

  • Guided Audio Sessions: A library of 94+ guided audio sessions covering anxiety management, panic attack support, intrusive thoughts, and relaxation techniques.
  • Daily Journal: A personal journaling tool for tracking your mental health, moods, and progress.
  • Exposure Therapy Logging: Tools to plan, track, and reflect on exposure therapy exercises.
  • 7-Day Challenges: Structured challenge programs to build resilience and coping skills.
  • SOS Mode: Quick-access tools for managing acute anxiety or panic episodes.
  • Playlists: Curated collections of audio sessions organized by topic and need.
  • Offline Downloads: The ability to download audio content for offline listening.
