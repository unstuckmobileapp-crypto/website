FAQ

Common questions

Is Unstuck a replacement for therapy?

No. Unstuck is designed to complement professional treatment, not replace it. The tools are based on evidence-based approaches like ACT and exposure therapy, but we always recommend working with a qualified therapist for personalized care.

Is the app free?

Yes! You get full access to all 94+ audio sessions, SOS mode, and 10 free journal entries. Subscribers get unlimited journaling, exposure logs, habit tracking, medicine tracking, and personalized evaluations.

What tracking tools are included?

Unstuck includes a daily mood journal, exposure therapy journal, habit tracker, and medicine tracker. All tools are designed to work together to give you and your therapist a complete picture of your mental health journey.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. Your data is encrypted, stored securely, and never sold to third parties. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app settings.

Can I use Unstuck offline?

Yes. You can download audio sessions for offline listening. Journal, habit, and exposure entries are saved locally and synced when you're back online.

What types of anxiety does Unstuck help with?

Unstuck covers general anxiety, panic attacks, health anxiety, social anxiety, intrusive thoughts, anticipatory anxiety, agoraphobia, driving anxiety, flying anxiety, and insomnia related to anxiety.
